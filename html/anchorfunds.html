AnchorFunds

2025

Context

AnchorFunds is a conceptual fintech product developed as part of an academic design project, exploring how education-first experiences can reduce financial anxiety and improve long-term investing outcomes for young adults.

The Insight

Users did not need another digital portfolio, they needed somebody to teach them how to properly do their finances without expensive fees or complicated modules.

1. Users don’t need to invest sooner.

2. They need structure, clarity, and reassurance before being asked to take financial risks.

Market Growth Projections (2025 → 2035)

To validate the opportunity and understand the broader landscape, we evaluated market projections for financial literacy and personalized finance apps. These numbers highlighted a significant gap: while the market is growing rapidly, few solutions address the confidence and learning needs of young adults before investing.

Financial literacy apps
2025: 5.27B

Personalized finance apps

The Solution

AnchorFunds guides users from financial fundamentals to investing readiness through a structured, staged learning experience. Instead of encouraging immediate action, the product deliberately slows users down:

1. Stage One: Safety Net

Users must save 3–6 months of expenses before progressing, establishing stability and reducing anxiety.

2. Stage Two: Learn Before Acting

Core financial concepts are taught through short, guided lessons using familiar learning patterns.

3. Stage Three: Confident Action

Users graduate into investing through a Wealthsimple partnership once they’re informed and ready.

Why This Works

AnchorFunds leverages HCI principles to create a clear, confidence-building learning experience for young adults:

  • Lower cognitive load through progressive disclosure and familiar mental models
  • Trust through restraint by delaying investing until users are ready
  • Clear progression replaces overwhelming dashboards with clarity
  • Mutual value for users and partners through education-first onboarding

Outcome & Impact

AnchorFunds reframes financial literacy as a prerequisite to investing rather than an optional add-on. The platform reduces anxiety around money, builds sustainable habits, and helps users transition into investing with confidence.

Most investment platforms assume prior knowledge. Financial jargon, dense dashboards, and fear of making mistakes prevent young adults from participating meaningfully in investing.
This isn’t a tools problem — it’s a confidence problem.

Reflection

This project reinforced that effective financial products don’t need to push users toward action as quickly as possible. In fact, slowing users down — when done intentionally — can build trust, confidence, and better long-term outcomes.

Rather than solving for feature completeness, AnchorFunds prioritizes clarity, pacing, and readiness. Designing with restraint proved more impactful than adding complexity, especially in high-anxiety domains like personal finance.

Key Takeaways

1. Design for confidence before action

2. Restraint can be a feature

3. Clarity beats completeness

If extended further, the next iteration would focus on adapting learning depth dynamically based on user confidence, allowing the experience to remain supportive without becoming prescriptive.
